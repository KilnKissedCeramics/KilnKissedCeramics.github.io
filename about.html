Hi, I'm Bhavya

The ceramic artist behind Kiln Kissed Ceramics, based in Sunnyvale, CA. I create small-batch, handmade pottery that blends function with beauty.

My work is rooted in a love of vibrant glazes, rich textures, and organic details that give each piece its own unique character. No two pieces are ever exactly the same—each one tells its own story through handmade charm and subtle variations.

What I love most about handmade ceramics is this very uniqueness: where every piece is full of character, just like the people who use them.
